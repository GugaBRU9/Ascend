Definir `option(ASCEND_ENABLE_SANITIZERS "Enable AddressSanitizer and UBSan" OFF)` e dois targets `INTERFACE`: `ascend_build_options` e `ascend_build_warnings`. Em GNU/Clang, aplicar `-Wall -Wextra -Wpedantic` em `ascend_build_warnings`; em MSVC aplicar `/W4 /permissive-`. Quando `ASCEND_ENABLE_SANITIZERS=ON`, aplicar `-fsanitize=address,undefined -fno-omit-frame-pointer` em GNU/Clang e emitir `message(FATAL_ERROR "asan-ubsan preset requires Clang or GCC")` fora desse caso. Adicionar `add_subdirectory(src)` e `if(BUILD_TESTING) add_subdirectory(tests) endif()`. Criar `src/CMakeLists.txt` com `if(EXISTS .../domain/CMakeLists.txt) add_subdirectory(domain) endif()` e equivalentes para `content`, `session` e `adapters`. Criar `tests/CMakeLists.txt` com placeholder seguro que emite `message(STATUS "No phase-2 tests registered yet")` quando `tests/bootstrap_smoke.cpp` ainda nao existir. Criar `CMakePresets.json` com presets de configure/build/test chamados exatamente `dev`, `asan-ubsan` e `release`, usando `binaryDir` em `build/dev`, `build/asan-ubsan` e `build/release`; o preset `asan-ubsan` deve definir `ASCEND_ENABLE_SANITIZERS=true`. Criar `.clang-format` baseado em LLVM com `IndentWidth: 2` e `ColumnLimit: 100`, e `.clang-tidy` com checks pequenos para `clang-analyzer-*`, `bugprone-*`, `performance-*`, `readability-*`, desabilitando ao menos `readability-magic-numbers`.</action>
